# User Manual
## Mindful Moments — AI Journaling App

**Version:** 4.0
**Last updated:** July 2026

---

## What is Mindful Moments?

Mindful Moments is a private journaling app that helps you reflect on your thoughts and emotions through a short, guided check-in. Instead of staring at a blank page, the app walks you through a few simple questions and then generates a warm, personalised reflection using AI.

Every entry is saved privately to your account. You can look back at past entries and see how your mood has changed over time on a dashboard.

**This app is not therapy.** It is a journaling companion. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a professional or call iCall India at **9152987821**.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Enter your email address and a password
3. Click **Sign Up**
4. Check your email inbox — you will receive a confirmation email
5. Click the confirmation link in the email
6. Return to the app and click **Log In**

**Note:** You must confirm your email before you can log in. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

---

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **Log In**
3. You will be taken directly to the journaling page

**Wrong password?** You will see: *"Incorrect email or password. Try again."*

---

### Logging Out

Click the **Log Out** button in the top right corner of the navigation bar.

## Writing a Journal Entry

The journaling flow has 4 phases. The whole check-in takes about 2 minutes.

### Phase 1 — How Are You Feeling?

You will see 5 mood options:

| Emoji | Mood |
|---|---|
| 😞 | Very low |
| 😕 | Low |
| 😐 | Neutral |
| 🙂 | Good |
| 😄 | Very good |

Tap one emoji to select your mood. This is required.

---

### Phase 2 — What Are You Feeling?

Select up to 3 emotions from: Anxious · Frustrated · Sad · Overwhelmed · Grateful · Hopeful · Excited · Calm · Confused · Proud · Lonely · Tired

At least one emotion is required.

---

### Phase 3 — A Little More Context

Two questions will appear based on the emotions you selected. These are optional — write as much or as little as you like.

---

### Phase 4 — Free Write + Mode

**Free write (optional):** Write anything else on your mind.

**Journaling mode:** Choose the style of reflection you want:

| Mode | Best for |
|---|---|
| Daily Reflection | General everyday check-in |
| Gratitude Journal | Focusing on the positive |
| Anxiety Dump | When you feel overwhelmed |
| Work Reflection | Processing work challenges |
| Decision Clarity | When stuck on a choice |

Default is **Daily Reflection**.

---

### Submitting Your Entry

Click **Reflect**. The button will change to **Thinking...** while the AI generates your reflection.

---

### Reading Your Reflection

Your reflection card will show:

- **Title** — a short 6-word summary
- **Mood note** — one validating sentence
- **Summary** — 2-3 sentences of reflection
- **Sentiment** — positive, neutral, negative, or mixed
- **Emotions detected** — 2-4 emotion words
- **Themes** — 2-3 themes from your entry
- **Questions to sit with** — 3 gentle follow-up questions
- **One small thing you can do** — a single action for today
- **Stoic quote** — a relevant philosophical quote

Your entry is automatically saved after the reflection appears.

---

## Past Entries

Click **Past Entries** in the nav bar to see your journal history.

- Last 20 entries shown, newest first
- Each card shows date, mood emoji, emotions, and AI title
- Click any card to expand and read the full reflection
- Click again to collapse

---

## Dashboard

Click **Dashboard** in the nav bar to see your mood over time.

**Stat boxes:** Total entries, entries this week, most-used mode

**Mood timeline chart:** Bar chart of your mood scores (1 = Very low, 5 = Very good) across your last entries

**Top emotions:** Your 5 most-used emotion chips with frequency counts

**Note:** Needs at least 3 entries to display.

## If Something Goes Wrong

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| "Something went wrong" after Reflect | Wait a moment and click Try again |
| Confirmation email not received | Check your spam folder |
| Can't log in | Make sure email is confirmed and credentials are correct |
| Past Entries not loading | Try refreshing the page |
| Dashboard not showing | You need at least 3 journal entries |
| App shows login screen unexpectedly | Your session expired — log in again |

---

## Privacy and Safety

- Your entries are **private to your account only**
- The app will **never** diagnose you with a mental health condition
- If you write about self-harm or crisis, the app shows a support message with the iCall India helpline: **9152987821**
- Your Gemini API key is never exposed in the browser — it stays on the server only
